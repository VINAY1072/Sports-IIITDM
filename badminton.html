Serve It, Smash It and Win It!

When you sweat with a happy heart you build up a healthy you. Badminton is a fun active sport which is an easy game to practice with few painless rules. The tiny white cock may deceive you with its meek look but moves like a swift, fast and furious!


An indoor badminton court with five synthetic courts is available in the IIITDM Arjuna Sports Complex. The institute is dedicated to teaching badminton to players and preparing them for competitions at the intercollegiate, state, and national levels. The institute badminton team has won a number of awards at Inter IIIT Sports Conclaves conducted at various IIITs, including IIITDM Jabalpur, IIIT Allahabad, and others. They attempt to put their opponents to the test and develop a sense of sportsmanship in the audience.


The IIITDM Sports Department hosts a variety of badminton competitions, including Shuttle Whackers, Novice Freshie League, and Squid Games Tournaments. This encourages children to incorporate sports into their daily routine, which might help them build sportsmanship perspective.
